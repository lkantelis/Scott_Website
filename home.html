Aplura offers a great learning environment where I am pushed to grow and develop my skills as an IT professional.

In my spare time, I work towards studying for certifications to prepare for my career in information security. Over the past year of working with Aplura, through self-studying and the help of my supportive coworkers, I was able to pass the Red Hat Certified System Administrator certification. I am now studying for the Security+ exam to learn more about the fundamentals of IT security. In the future I plan on obtaining the RHCE - Red Hat Certified Engineer - and CISSP - Certified Information Systems Security Professional.
