About Me

My hometown, Westminster, MD, is not exactly the hub for technology. Typical Carroll County, Maryland activities included hunting, farming, and tractor driving. As you can imagine, there is little advanced technology in a place like Westminster.

At first I did not expect to enter the IT field. In high school, I excelled at the French Horn and planned to become a music performer for a symphony. I made the Maryland All-State Orchestra three years in a row and was the highest ranked performer in two of those years. This accomplishment allowed me to performance with the All-Eastern Orchestra and All-National Band which held some of the best musicians from around the country. Since I am not currently majoring in music, I participate in Towson University’s Symphonic Band for pure enjoyment.

Along with music, I enjoy playing soccer. I’ve played soccer since I was in elementary school and was captain of my high school team. Since I don’t have the time now to continue playing soccer, I simply watch it when I get the chance.

Even though music and soccer are my hobbies, they can be a little stressful at times. However, my greatest relaxation is snowboarding. I have snowboarded up in the North East region and will be going out West to Colorado in the Spring. This is definitely my way of keeping calm when life gets a little stressful, so I look forward to the Winter every year.
